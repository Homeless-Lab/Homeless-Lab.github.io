A Turn-Based MMORPG Chat-Based Game

What is Prompt-Star-Fantasy?

A turn-based MMORPG chat-based game with three waifus. The game features chat mode, battle mode, and town mode. Waifus can speak, listen to your voice, send images (?????), and interact with each other (though this may take time to process).

All you must do is just prompt!

Hashtag: #Open-Source, #local-inference, #RAG

🌟 Prompt-Star-Fantasy (Open-Source)

    • ⚔️ Battle Mode Mechanics

    In battle mode, three agents enhance gameplay:

    • Valuate Agent
    • Summarizer
    • Generator

    The boss monster is randomly generated based on the map's characteristics.

    Battle Tips
  • Ask your party about their skills before the battle.
  • Bosses can have weaknesses—ask your party.
  • Manage your party by calling their names before issuing commands.
  • 🚚 Fun fact: Aeliana can drive Trucksung to attack bosses!

    • 🏘️ Town Mode

    You can flirt and interact with your party in the town!
